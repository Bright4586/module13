Enjoy Our Specialities

Entrees include our quarter-pound hot dog, a 16-inch pizza or just a slice, or a pizza pretzel served with a cup of marinara sauce for dipping. Snacks include salted or cinnamon sugar pretzels, also available with a nacho cheese cup.
For cold snacks, try our yogurt cups with vanilla, chocolate, or swirl flavors. Try our 4-berry sundaes with raspberry, strawberry, blueberry, or blackberry with vanilla or chocolate, or a brownie sundae with caramel sauce drizzled over vanilla frozen yogurt and topped with chunks of chocolate brownie from our bakery.

We’ve also got Grab N’ Go Caesar salads and fruit.
